gerbage power plant

The waste power plant is a thermal power plant with steam supercritical steam and fueled by waste or methane waste gas. Rubbish or waste methane burns produce heat that heats up steam in a supercritical steam boiler. High vapor compression then drives the steam and flywheel turbines that are connected to the dynamo generator with an intermediate transmission gear or automatic transmission to generate electricity. The power generated at this plant varies from 500 KW to 10 MW. Compare with coal-fired power plant with power 40 MW to 100 MW per unit or nuclear power plant powered 300 MW to 1200 MW per unit.

THE CONCEPT OF EMPLOYMENT OF ELECTRICAL POWER PLANT

In general, the work concept of Waste Power Generation is as follows:
1. Sorting garbage
Trash is sorted to use recyclable waste. The rest of the waste is put in the Incinerator stove to be burned.
2. Garbage burning
Burning waste using combustion technology that enables effective and safe running for the environment. The combustion temperature is maintained in a high degree of combustion (above 300 Â° C). The flue coming out of the combustion is also controlled to conform to the standard emission standard
3. Utilization of heat
The result of burning waste will generate heat that can be utilized to heat the boiler. The resulting heat generator is used to rotate the turbine and then move the electrical generator.
